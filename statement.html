Artist Statement

I want my art to be rather light-hearted and relatable. I'm fine with incorporating messages and statements into my art but overall I don't want it to be taken too seriously and would rather everyone have fun and get a laugh or two out of my works. I do try to have a vision in mind but very rarely do I overcomplicate it.
My interests are in anime, games, and pop culture in general. I aim to incorporate these aspects of my life into my work as often as possible.

“Every artist was first an amateur.” ~ Ralph Waldo Emerson.
And I'm the biggest amateur there is.
